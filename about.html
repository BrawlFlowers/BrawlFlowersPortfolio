About Me

Bio

Angel Flores is an emerging multimedia artist who holds an Associates Degree in Fine arts from Riverside City College and a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Digital Media arts from San Jose State University. He has a focus towards character deisng and visual development for the fields of video games and animation. He is inspired and intrigued by the visual development stages that occur within large collaborative projects. His range of skills vary from creating and iterating on character and environmental prop designs, assisting in animations, and illustrating and rendering assets that will be used in-game.
